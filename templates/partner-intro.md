# Partner Introduction Template

A 10-slide structure for introducing your company to potential partners.

## Template Structure

Use this structure when creating partner introductions. Each section becomes a slide.

```
[Title]
[Company Name] + [Partner Name]
Partnership Opportunity
---
[Who We Are]
About [Company Name]
- Mission/vision
- What we do
- Key differentiators
---
[What We've Built]
Our products and capabilities
- Core platform/product
- Key features
- Technology stack
---
[Our Customers]
Who we serve
- Target segments
- Notable customers
- Use cases
---
[Why Partner]
The opportunity for collaboration
- Complementary strengths
- Shared customers
- Market opportunity
---
[Partnership Model]
How we can work together
- Integration options
- Revenue/value share
- Support model
---
[Success Stories]
What partnerships have achieved
- Existing partner examples
- Results and metrics
- Testimonials
---
[Next Steps]
Moving forward together
- Pilot proposal
- Timeline
- Key milestones
---
[Team]
Your partnership contacts
- Key people
- Roles and responsibilities
- Communication channels
---
[Contact]
Let's build something together
- Contact information
- Meeting request
- Resources
```

## Usage Notes

### Tone
- Collaborative, not sales-y
- Focus on mutual value
- Professional and respectful

### Customization Points
- Replace [placeholders] with partner-specific content
- Emphasize synergies relevant to this partner
- Include partner's logo/branding references

### Partner-Specific Research
Before creating, gather:
- Partner's products/services
- Overlap with your customers
- Potential integration points
- Their partnership program (if any)

## Gamma Parameters

When using this template:
```json
{
  "textMode": "generate",
  "numCards": 10,
  "textOptions": {
    "tone": "collaborative, professional, win-win",
    "audience": "business development, executives, technical leads"
  }
}
```
